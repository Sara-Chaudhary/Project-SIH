Why Third Eye ?

With Third Eye , you can finally take control of your online world. We utilize state-of-the-art artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to analyze profiles across various platforms, identifying telltale signs of fraud, deception, or impersonation. Our application scans for inconsistencies in profile photos, unusual behavior patterns, and suspicious account activities to spot fake profiles with remarkable accuracy.

Profile Verification :


Third Eye verifies the authenticity of user profiles, helping you ensure that the people you interact with are who they claim to be.

Real-time Scanning :


Our application constantly monitors your online community, providing real-time alerts and updates on suspicious profiles, so you can take action swiftly.

Easy Reporting:


Reporting a fake profile is as simple as a few taps. We provide a seamless reporting system that notifies platform administrators and takes action against the fraudulent accounts.

Privacy Protection:


Your data security and privacy are paramount to us. Third Eye operates with strict data protection measures to safeguard your personal information.
